##Subject and Activity

####Subject

Data from 30 volunteers between an age 19-48 years

###Activity

Individuals involved in the experiment performed 6 different activities, by wearing smartphone (Samsung Galaxy) 

The activites were:
⋅⋅* WALKING
⋅⋅* WALKING_UPSTAIRS
⋅⋅* WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
⋅⋅* SITTING
⋅⋅* STANDING
⋅⋅* LAYING
